## 1 官网下载

网址：https://www.jetbrains.com/idea/

![image-20221121103934371](https://my-typora-photos.oss-cn-beijing.aliyuncs.com/img/202211211039480.png)

选择`Download`，Mac和Windows按照自己的需求选择，一般使用社区免费版即可

![image-20221121104043904](https://my-typora-photos.oss-cn-beijing.aliyuncs.com/img/202211211040406.png)

下载可能需要注册，不需要理会，等待下载界面出来就行了

## 2 安装IDEA

双击下载好的ideaIU-2018.3.4.exe文件（ideaIU-2022.2.3.dmg），选择next

![img](https://my-typora-photos.oss-cn-beijing.aliyuncs.com/img/202211211042146.png)

Windows电脑需要自定义设置安装目录，Mac电脑的话无需设置目录，一直下一步即可

![img](https://my-typora-photos.oss-cn-beijing.aliyuncs.com/img/202211211043565.png)

按照下面的勾选就行了

![img](https://my-typora-photos.oss-cn-beijing.aliyuncs.com/img/202211211043618.png)

点击install，等待安装成功就行了

![img](https://my-typora-photos.oss-cn-beijing.aliyuncs.com/img/202211211043291.png)
